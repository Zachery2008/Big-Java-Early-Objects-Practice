Arithmetic Operations

  • Operators: + - *
    10 + n
    n - 1
    10 * n       // 10 × n
  • As in mathematics, the * operator binds more strongly than the + operator
    x + y * 2    // means the sum of x and y * 2
    (x + y) * 2  // multiplies the sum of x and y with 2
