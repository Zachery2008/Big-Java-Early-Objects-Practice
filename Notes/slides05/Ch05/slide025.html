Drawing Lines

  • To draw a line: 
    Line2D.Double segment = new Line2D.Double(x1, y1, x2, y2);
    or, 
    Point2D.Double from = new Point2D.Double(x1, y1);
    Point2D.Double to = new Point2D.Double(x2, y2);
    Line2D.Double segment = new Line2D.Double(from, to);
