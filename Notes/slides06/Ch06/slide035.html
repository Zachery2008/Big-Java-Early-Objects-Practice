Using Boolean Variables

  • private boolean married;
  • Set to truth value:
    married = input.equals("M");
  • Use in conditions:
    if (married) . . . else . . .
    if (!married) . . .
  • Also called flag
  • It is considered gauche to write a test such as
    if (married == true) . . . // Don't
    Just use the simpler test
    if (married) . . .
