Common Errors: Semicolons

  • A semicolon that shouldn't be there
    sum = 0;
    for (i = 1; i <= 10; i++);
       sum = sum + i;
    System.out.println(sum);
  • A missing semicolon 
    for (years = 1; (balance = balance + balance * rate / 100) < targetBalance; years++)
    System.out.println(years);
