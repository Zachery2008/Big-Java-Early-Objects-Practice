Other Ways to Compute Triangle Numbers

  • The area of a triangle equals the sum
    1 + 2 + 3 + . . . + width
  • Using a simple loop: 
    double area = 0;
    for (int i = 1; i <= width; i++)
       area = area + i;
  • Using math: 
    1 + 2 + . . . + n = n × (n + 1)/2
    => width * (width + 1) / 2
