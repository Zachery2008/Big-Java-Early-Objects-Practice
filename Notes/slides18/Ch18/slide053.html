Using Mutual Recursions

  • Problem: to compute the value of arithmetic expressions such as

    3 + 4 * 5
    (3 + 4) * 5
    1 - (2 - (3 - (4 - 5)))

  • Computing expression is complicated
    • * and / bind more strongly than + and -
    • parentheses can be used to group subexpressions
